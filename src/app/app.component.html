Use these tools to highlight words in the card text area. (For example, like how Double Strike is generally in red.)
If you want to highlight only standard keywords in the game, you may just hit the "Auto" button.
If you want to highlight your own keyword skills, you may highlight the word in the text area above and then hit one of the colored circular buttons.
If you want to use a custom color, you may enter the hex code in the box, select your word, and hit "Go!".
To clear highlighting, hit the green trash can button.
